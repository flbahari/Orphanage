Our Activities

At Rumah Perlindungan Nur Qaseh, we strive to nurture, educate, and empower children through a variety of meaningful activities that support their growth and well-being.

Educational Support

  • Daily tuition and homework assistance
  • Islamic studies and moral education classes
  • Computer literacy and language learning sessions

Recreational Activities

  • Sports such as football and badminton
  • Arts and crafts workshops
  • Group storytelling and reading time

Religious Activities

  • Daily prayers and Quranic recitations
  • Religious talks and motivational sessions
  • Celebrations of Islamic festivals like Eid and Maulidur Rasul

Health and Wellness

  • Regular health check-ups and vaccinations
  • Nutritional meal programs
  • Hygiene and personal care workshops

Community Engagement

  • Charity and donation drives
  • Volunteer programs and community service
  • Visits to hospitals and elderly homes

Life Skills Development

  • Basic cooking and housekeeping skills
  • Time management and discipline training
  • Counseling and emotional support sessions
